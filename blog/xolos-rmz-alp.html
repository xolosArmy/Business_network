Xolos RMZ Migrates from SLP to ALP

A new chapter for the XolosArmy eToken ecosystem: migrating Xolos RMZ to the modern ALP standard on eCash, unlocking stronger infrastructure, DEX integrations and long-term growth.

Date: 30 November 2025 · Project: XolosArmy Network – Xolos RMZ eToken

XolosArmy Network is taking an important step forward in the evolution of its community token. We are officially migrating Xolos RMZ from the legacy SLP standard to the new ALP-based eToken standard on eCash.

From now on, all development, integrations and utilities will use the new ALP token:

The previous SLP-based version of RMZ is now considered “legacy” and will no longer be the focus of new features.

Why We Are Moving from SLP to ALP

The XolosArmy vision is to build a long-term cultural and technological ecosystem around the xoloitzcuintli: NFTs, DeFi, independent media, and community tools. For that, we need a token standard that is:

  • Native to modern eCash infrastructure
  • Efficient to index and query for Chronik and Agora
  • Well supported by current wallets and tooling

SLP served the early stages of our project, but it has several limitations for the roadmap we are building:

1. Tooling and ecosystem have moved to ALP

New infrastructure, such as Cashtab’s eToken implementation, indexers like Chronik, and smart-contract layers such as Agora, are designed to work with ALP eTokens as first-class citizens. This makes ALP the realistic standard for future integrations.

2. Better compatibility with DEX and advanced features

Our plans for a non-custodial XEC ↔ RMZ DEX, order-book or AMM functionality, and other on-chain logic are designed around ALP + Chronik + Agora. Running these on SLP would be fragile or impossible; on ALP it becomes straightforward and maintainable.

3. Cleaner, long-term token identity

Migrating now allows us to establish one canonical RMZ token ID on the modern standard, avoiding confusion with multiple parallel contracts and giving the community a clear reference for what is “real RMZ”.

In short: SLP was the first chapter; ALP is the format that lets Xolos RMZ grow into its full potential.
What Exactly Is Changing?

1. A New Canonical Token

The ALP version of Xolos RMZ is a fresh token genesis with:

  • A fixed maximum supply of 21,000,000 RMZ, mirroring the symbolic scarcity of 21 million Bitcoin.
  • Two decimal places, making RMZ intuitive to use for prices, tips, and rewards.
  • A permanent link to our official website, so explorers and wallets can easily identify the token as part of XolosArmy Network.

From now on, whenever we say “Xolos RMZ”, we are referring to this ALP token.

2. The SLP Version Becomes Legacy

The old SLP-based RMZ:

  • Will no longer receive new utilities or integrations.
  • Is effectively frozen in time and treated as a historical artifact of the early phase of the project.
  • Exists only as a reference for the migration snapshot used to allocate the new ALP tokens.

The economic and cultural value of the project now lives in the ALP token.

What This Means for Holders

If you were already holding Xolos RMZ in its SLP form, the migration is designed to respect your position and your support for the project.

The migration process follows three principles:

1. Snapshot of SLP Holders

A snapshot of SLP balances at a specific block height is (or will be) taken. This snapshot defines who is entitled to receive RMZ on ALP and in what amount.

2. Allocation of ALP RMZ

The new total supply of 21,000,000 RMZ ALP is used to:

  • Match SLP balances (for example at a 1:1 ratio, or another clearly documented ratio), and
  • Allocate a defined portion to the community treasury, development fund, and ecosystem rewards.

3. Simple User Experience

The goal is that most holders do not need to take any complex action:

  • You simply keep control of your wallet.
  • You receive the new RMZ ALP according to the published migration rules.
  • You use RMZ ALP going forward in all XolosArmy tools.

Detailed instructions (including block height, snapshot date, and distribution ratio) will be published on xolosarmy.xyz and through our official communication channels (Telegram, Twitter/X, etc.).

Benefits for the XolosArmy Ecosystem

1. Stronger Infrastructure for RMZWallet / Tonalli

Our community wallet (RMZWallet / “Tonalli”) is being built around:

  • Full nodes and indexers for eCash
  • Chronik for on-chain queries
  • Agora for advanced smart-contract logic and DEX features

All of this is designed with ALP eTokens in mind. Standardizing on ALP means:

  • More reliable balances and history for RMZ users
  • Faster and more accurate on-chain analytics
  • The ability to plug RMZ into non-custodial trading, liquidity pools, and programmable incentives

In practical terms, it means RMZ will work better, faster and more safely in the apps that matter.

2. Cleaner Integration with DEX and On-Chain Markets

With a single, canonical ALP token ID for RMZ:

  • We can set up XEC/RMZ trading pairs on smart-contract-based DEX infrastructure.
  • The market can clearly distinguish the real RMZ from impostors or look-alike tokens.
  • Builders can integrate RMZ with confidence, knowing they are using the official contract.

This is essential for the long-term vision of XolosArmy as a cultural + financial network, not just a fan token.

3. Security, Transparency and Governance

A fixed supply of 21,000,000 RMZ on ALP reinforces several values:

  • Transparency: everyone can verify total supply and circulating amounts on-chain.
  • Predictability: no hidden minting; tokenomics are clear and auditable.
  • Governance-ready: ALP tokens can become the foundation for future community voting, treasury management and incentive programs without worrying about legacy quirks.
