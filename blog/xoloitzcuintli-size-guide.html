The Xoloitzcuintle, or "Xolo," is more than a dog; it's an emblem of Mexican culture with over 3,500 years of history. Revered by the Mexica (Aztecs) as a spiritual guide, this ancient and loyal breed comes in three sizes. Each size shares the same noble, calm temperament but has unique needs and advantages that suit different homes and families. This interactive guide helps you explore each size’s traits to find your ideal companion.
